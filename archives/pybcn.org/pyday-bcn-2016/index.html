PyDay BCN 2016

We are excited and glad to announce that we are organizing our first big event related to Python. PyDay BCN 2016 is going to take place in Barcelona on 15 and 17 September: two days of FREE different and funny activities about Python.

We count with an incredible group of volunteers, sponsors, and contributing entities and companies. Thanks to all them.

We really hope you'll have a great time attending to these!!
